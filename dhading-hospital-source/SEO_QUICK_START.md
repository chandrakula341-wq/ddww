# SEO Quick-Start Checklist for Dhading Hospital

## Critical Actions (Do These First!)

### 1. Google Analytics Setup ⭐
- [ ] Get your Google Analytics 4 ID from https://analytics.google.com
- [ ] Open `index.html` and find line with `G-XXXXXXXXXX`
- [ ] Replace `XXXXXXXXXX` with your actual Analytics ID
- [ ] Verify tracking is working

### 2. Google Search Console ⭐
- [ ] Visit https://search.google.com/search-console
- [ ] Add/verify your domain: `dhadinghospital.com.np`
- [ ] Upload `public/sitemap.xml`
- [ ] Wait for Google to crawl (24-48 hours)

### 3. Google My Business ⭐
- [ ] Create/verify Google My Business listing

- [ ] Set operating hours (24/7)
- [ ] Add services/specialties

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't:**
- Copy content from other hospitals
- Use keyword stuffing (too many keywords)
- Buy backlinks or followers
- Hide content from users
- Use automated link-building services
- Spam comments/forums
- Cloak your website
- Create doorway pages

**These practices can get you banned from Google!**
